Plan Compliance Portal

I designed and implemented a Plan Compliance Portal to streamline the annual compliance process for retirement plan sponsors. Each year, sponsors must verify firm-level details and ensure the accuracy of employee data in our retirement recordkeeping systems.

This portal simplifies the process by:

  • Sending secure, personalized invitations with magic sign-in links for each plan sponsor.
  • Guiding sponsors through a dynamic questionnaire to capture any changes to firm ownership or operations.
  • Presenting a data validation interface that displays employee-level details such as total hours worked, compensation, pre-tax contributions, etc.
  • Automatically identifying discrepancies in the data and allowing sponsors to either correct them directly or provide override explanations.

The system improves data integrity, reduces compliance risk, and minimizes friction for plan sponsors during year-end audits.

Onboarding Portal

After the success of the Plan Compliance Portal, I extended the software to support a fully integrated Plan Onboarding Portal for new client implementations.

This system automates and streamlines the onboarding process by:

  • Sending secure, branded invitations to new clients with guided access to onboarding steps.
  • Walking clients through a structured implementation questionnaire to gather plan details.
  • Facilitating employee census data uploads for accurate plan setup.
  • Allowing clients to schedule their plan design call with the implementations team.
  • Providing the internal team with a centralized dashboard to manage onboarding projects end-to-end.

The portal enables our implementations team to oversee all aspects of onboarding - from payroll integration, custodian account setup, and fund company approvals, all the way through to handoff to the platform operations team - ensuring consistency, visibility, and operational efficiency.

Automatic IRA Product

I led the architecture and development of the GROUPIRA Automatic IRA Product, a scalable SaaS solution that streamlines the automatic rollover of retirement plan accounts for over 100+ partner firms nationwide. When employees leave a company, employers often need to transfer their retirement balances into individual IRAs to reduce plan costs and maintain compliance. GROUPIRA automates this end-to-end process with a secure, integrated platform.

Key capabilities include:

  • Secure data intake via file upload or SFTP, supporting participant census submissions from partner firms.
  • LexisNexis integration to verify and enrich participant address data through lost participant searches.
  • Automated compliance notifications, including 30-day distribution notices sent to participants via mail.
  • Funds reconciliation and IRA account creation upon rollover initiation - integrated with our recordkeeping systems.
  • Support for custom document workflows using DocuSign for Plan Sponsor agreements.
  • Dynamic handling of Plan Sponsor communications, custom mailing inserts, and exception processing.

GROUPIRA manages the full lifecycle of automatic IRA force-outs, delivering operational efficiency, compliance, and scale across a complex financial workflow.
