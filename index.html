YTD Rate of Return (ROR)

I customized the ROR bubble on the account balance dashboard widget to calculate a full YTD ROR, when available. Contributions Step - Online Enrollment

Through my research talking with Advisors, we found that many Participants were getting confused on the contributions step of online participant enrollment. For example, during enrollment meetings, Participants would get stuck trying to figure out how to use the action table - especially on mobile. If a Participant is enrolling on their phone, they would have to scroll horizontally on the action table with their thumb. We completely redesigned this page to not only be much more mobile-friendly, but also to give suggested contribution amounts to increase participation. The introductory text on this page is dynamic, and changes based on the Participant's current deferral elections.

ABG Advantage IRA - Online Account Origination:

Users are able to visit abgrm.com/ira to instantly sign up for an ABG Advantage IRA account. The user can fill out the entire application, designate beneficiaries, take a risk assessment to determine their investment portfolio, setup initial contributions or rollover amounts, e-sign agreements, and more. A summary PDF deliverable is then generated and transmitted to both the user, as well as GROUPIRA. GROUPIRA can login to the website to download the application and import into their Relius systems.



ABGRM - Secure File Upload Portal

ABGRM clients are able to easily upload their secure files such as distribution forms, contribution files, conversion documents, etc. The file is then automatically transmitted securely to the applicable team members at ABGRM.
